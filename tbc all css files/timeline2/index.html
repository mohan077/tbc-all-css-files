1993

Norvic Health care & Research Centre Pvt.Ltd. starts its services with a 30 bedded facility.

1997

MoU was signed between Norvic International Hospital and Escorts Heart Institute, New Delhi to develop and Heart Command Centre was established.

1999

Centre for Neurological & Allied Sciences was established and Neurosurgical procedures started.

2002

Norvic gets certified with ISO 9001:2000 (certification for quality management system)

2004

Obstetrics & Gynecology department was established.

2005

Urology speciality and CT scan facility was started.

2006

Norvic carried out its first total knee replacement surgery

2008

Smt. Ganga Devi Chaudhary Cath Lab established

2013

A dedicated area for gynecology speciality was established.

2016

State-of-the-art JCI standard Modular Operating Suites started.

2017

Lunkarandas Chaudhary Diagnostic Centre innagurated. Norvic now provides all diagnostic facilities under one roof.

2020

Norvic launches Covid Fever Clinic, Covid isolation ward & Covid ICU.

2023

Construction for new wing started
